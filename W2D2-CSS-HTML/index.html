As a token of my appreciation, here's a secret recipe belonging to my grandmother. Enjoy!

Bistec

  • Beef Filets
    Thinkly Sliced
    (Recommended: Marinade in Mojo)
  • Onion
    (Sliced into Rings)
  • Bitter Orange Marinade
  • Garlic
    (Finely chopped)

Steps:

  1. Add onions to pan on medium heat
  2. As onions begin to caramelize, add filets
  3. Add bitter orange marinade to taste (Doesn't take much!)
  4. Flip filets once they are golden brown on the bottom (5-8 minutes)
  5. Add garlic to taste
  6. Finish by cooking the filets on their other side, again until they're golden brown
  7. Serve with choice of side/sides
  8. Enjoy! :)
